The **Personalized Career Roadmap Generator** is a simple interactive website that helps students figure out what steps to take to reach their career goa

Here's what it does from start to finish:

The user lands on the **home page**, which introduces the project and has a "Get Started" button to begin.

Clicking that takes them to the **form page**, where they fill in details about themselves — their name, education level, the career they want to pursue, their current skills, experience level, how many hours a day they can study, their budget, and how they prefer to learn.

Once they submit the form, the website reads all their answers and instantly generates a **personalized result page** with four things:

1. **A step-by-step roadmap** — a numbered list of things to learn and do, tailored to their chosen career and adjusted based on whether they are a beginner, intermediate, or advanced learner.
2. **Recommended resources** — links to YouTube channels, free websites, and practice platforms relevant to their career path.
3. **An estimated timeline** — calculated based on how many hours per day they can study, showing estimated weeks and months to complete the roadmap.
4. **A progress tracker** — an interactive checklist where they can tick off tasks as they complete them, with a live progress bar that fills up as they go.

There is also a **reset button** to start over and a **back button** to edit their answers. The whole thing runs in a single HTML file — no internet connection or server required, just open it in a browser.
